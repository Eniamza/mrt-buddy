Features

  • Instantly check your MRT Pass balance using NFC
  • View your last 10 transactions
  • Works with both MRT Pass and Rapid Pass
  • No internet connection required

How to Use

  1. Download and install the app
  2. Enable NFC on your device
  3. Place your MRT Pass or Rapid Pass on the back of your phone
  4. View your balance and transaction history instantly

Requirements

  • Android 5.0 or higher
  • NFC-capable device
  • Dhaka MRT Pass or Rapid Pass
